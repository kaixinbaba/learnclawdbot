# Moltbot Glossary

This guide explains key terms and concepts you will encounter when using Moltbot.

## Core Concepts

### Agent
An autonomous AI entity capable of executing tasks, making decisions, and interacting with various tools and environments.

### Skill
Specific abilities or functions that can be added to Moltbot. Skills extend Moltbot's capabilities, such as accessing new APIs, performing specific calculations, or integrating with other services.

### Workspace
The environment where Moltbot runs. A workspace contains your files, configurations, and context.

### Task
A specific unit of work assigned to Moltbot. Tasks can be simple (e.g., "Create a file") or complex (e.g., "Build a website").

## Advanced Terms

### Context Window
The amount of information (text, code, history) that Moltbot can consider at once when processing a request.

### Planner
The component in Moltbot responsible for breaking down high-level goals into executable steps.

## Community Terms

### ClawdHub
The official marketplace for browsing and installing Moltbot skills and extensions.
