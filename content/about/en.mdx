# About Learn OpenClaw

Welcome to **Learn OpenClaw**, your ultimate independent resource for mastering OpenClaw (formerly known as Clawbot and Moltbot).

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple: **To make advanced AI automation accessible to everyone.**

We believe that tools like OpenClaw have the power to transform how we work and live by automating the mundane, but the steep learning curve can often be a barrier. We're here to bridge that gap with clear, step-by-step tutorials, deep-dive comparisons, and a supportive community-driven approach.

## Our Story

Learn OpenClaw began in early 2026 when a group of AI enthusiasts and developers (who were early adopters of Clawbot) realized there was a lack of comprehensive, beginner-friendly documentation for the rapidly evolving OpenClaw ecosystem.

What started as a collection of personal notes and "cheat sheets" quickly grew into a full-fledged educational platform. We've tracked the project through its rebrandings—from the original **Clawbot**, to **Moltbot**, and finally to the robust **OpenClaw** it is today.

## Why We Are Independent

Learn OpenClaw is an **independent, third-party project**. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the official OpenClaw development team.

**Why does this matter?**
- **Unbiased Reviews:** We can provide honest comparisons with other tools (like Claude Code or AutoGPT).
- **User-Centric Tutorials:** Our guides are written from the perspective of a user, focusing on real-world problems and edge cases.
- **Community First:** We prioritize what the community wants to learn next.

## What We Offer

- **Step-by-Step Guides:** From installation to advanced multi-agent orchestration.
- **Case Studies:** Real-world examples of how users are automating their lives using OpenClaw.
- **Ecosystem Updates:** Stay informed about the latest plugins, skill manifests, and roadmap changes.
- **Glossary:** A comprehensive guide to AI agent terminology.

## Meet the Team
