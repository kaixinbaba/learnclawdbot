# What Was Clawbot?

Clawbot was the earliest name of the AI agent tool now known as **OpenClaw**. The product was later called Moltbot and is now OpenClaw.

## Name Timeline

- **Clawbot** → **Moltbot** → **OpenClaw**

For the latest product information, start here:

- [What Is OpenClaw?](/what-is-openclaw)

## Same Product, New Name

OpenClaw retains the core capabilities that Clawbot introduced:

- **Execute Tasks**: Perform real actions on your behalf
- **Understand Context**: Maintain awareness of your project and goals
- **Work Autonomously**: Complete complex multi-step tasks with minimal guidance
- **Learn and Adapt**: Improve over time based on your preferences
